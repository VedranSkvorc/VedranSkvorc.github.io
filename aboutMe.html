About Me

My name is Vedran Skvorc, originally from Croatia and currently based in Austria. I began my higher education journey with a BSc in Computing, IT and Business from The Open University and am currently pursuing an MSc in Computer Science at the University of Essex.

Professionally, I work as an Integration Engineer, developing system interfaces to connect enterprise systems. My work focuses primarily on e-invoicing and financial process integrations, particularly designing and implementing integrations between Transport Management Systems and SAP. I enjoy working at the intersection of business and technology, translating complex processes into scalable, efficient integration solutions.
